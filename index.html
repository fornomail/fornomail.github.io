International Business Development



We accelerate the identification, the capture and building of a structured, scalable and manageable growth of your international potential and presence.



We guide the strategic consideration of why and how to best develop your international presence and the implementation of sustainable operating plans. We identify consumers in new export markets, across industry sectors.



We are retained and project based advisors to private equity and operating company executives. Our goal is to reduce the time and cost to generate profitable results and to enhance your enterprise value from expansion.

WHAT WE DO

Scope

Whether embarking on new overseas expansion, diversification, considering new markets or needing to restructure under-performing areas of an existing international presence, we bring expertise to reinforce your resources. We advise private equity and mentor groups from the early stages through to the restructuring of existing operations.

We guide leadership and focus your team on the fundamentals of extending your reach. We will diagnose gaps in competencies and capacity across your footprint to attain your objectives and capture growth potential.

Our aim is to assist you to move smartly within the resource allocations supported by your business and partners. We take a thorough look at the options. We look at your brand, service and product lines, the positioning, marketing and channels to understand how they need to be tailored for new markets.

We spot financial and other resource constraints and help you incorporate strategies and plans into the organization to address these.

We are accomplished in actionable market analysis, assessing market entry options, partner selection, contract agreements, establishing distribution channels, pricing and legal structures and creating support functions.

We will guide the transition to culturally adapted marketing programs and the development of an effective commercial approach.

We support strategy sessions, mentoring, market analysis and research, business plan development and active implementation across the organization.

Outcomes

We take the long term view of international expansion: to prioritize viable opportunities, finding and supporting global partners who respect the value of collaboration in representing your brand.

We accelerate the capture of incremental volumes, margins or strategic advantage.

We transfer competency to your organization to maintain vibrant international operations and sales.

The goal is to generate enhanced enterprise value in a way that makes international presence a compelling and integral part of your business.

Patricio Forno

Download vCard

Prior to co-founding AccelerateIBD, he had the challenge to develop the international presence of Iredale Mineral Cosmetics, a leading manufacturer, marketer and distributor of branded cosmetics with multichannel distribution in the US and 29 overseas markets. Since 2011, the footprint was expanded to 50 countries and the share of international sales grew from 15% to over 30%. This included extensive work in Mexico and Brazil, across Latin America and Asia, especially Singapore and S. Korea, Pan-European pricing strategies and new brand launch in Scandinavia.

This sustained export sales growth earned the company the US President's “E” Award in 2016, the highest recognition for significant contribution to US exports. Patricio is a member of the District Export Council of Massachusetts (DEC), appointed under the U.S. Secretary of Commerce.

He studied Industrial Engineering at the Universidad de Chile and attended Business School at the Universidad Diego Portales, in his native Santiago. His Chilean professional life included founding a successful entrepreneurial venture selling fire prevention equipment, executive management of the country's largest wallcovering importer, distributing 15,000 products from Europe and Canada, and managing the operations and extensive supply chain of a leading wholesale and retail construction materials and equipment group.

Naturalized in the US, he has also directed the sales and operations of a home furnishings manufacturer and then ran his own financial trading enterprise for 5 years, developing proprietary data base management tools.

A lifetime motorcyclist and trained wilderness guide in Patagonia, he enjoys mountaineering and lives in Connecticut where he and his wife are avid paragliders.
